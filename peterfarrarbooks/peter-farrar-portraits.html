Portrait Photography in Basingstoke

Peter Farrar Portraits offers professional studio and location portrait photography in Basingstoke and across Hampshire. Founded in 2008 in Charlotte, North Carolina as Kenneth Light Studios, the business has spent eighteen years photographing people across the UK and USA — individuals, families, performers, executives and businesses.

Since relocating to the UK and rebranding as Peter Farrar Portraits, the same approach that built a reputation for five-star portrait work across the United States has been brought to Basingstoke. Unhurried sessions, honest communication, and portraits that genuinely reflect the people in them.

Whether you need confident professional headshots in Basingstoke, timeless family portraits in Hampshire, or personal branding photography for your business, every session is tailored entirely to you and delivered to the highest standard.

The photographer behind the lens

Peter Farrar is also a published author with five books on photography and leadership. His writing on photography comes from the same place as his portrait work: direct professional experience. The Complete Photography Field Guide, at 538 pages, is the most comprehensive single-volume photography reference available in the UK, covering technique, workflow, business and UK photography law.

The connection between the books and the portrait work is straightforward. The knowledge that goes into writing about photography at that level is the same knowledge applied in every portrait session. Understanding light, composition, the psychology of working with people in front of a camera, and the technical discipline to deliver consistently excellent results.
