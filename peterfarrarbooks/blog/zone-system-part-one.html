The Zone System Explained – Part One: Seeing Light Like Ansel Adams

Few names in photography carry the same weight as Ansel Adams. His black and white landscapes remain some of the most striking images ever made.

What Is the Zone System?

Developed in the 1930s as a way to control exposure and tonal range, the system allows you to translate the world into intentional shades.

Zone Tonal Description
Zone 0Pure black, no detail
Zone VMiddle grey
